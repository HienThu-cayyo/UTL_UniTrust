# UTL_UniTrust
link: https://github.com/HienThu-cayyo/UTL_UniTrust                                                                                      ## 📝 Project DescriptionUniTrust Ledger is a decentralized application (dApp) built on the Stellar network using Soroban Smart Contracts. It is designed to revolutionize how academic achievements—specifically GPA and Training Points—are stored, shared, and verified.In the current educational landscape, verifying credentials is often slow, paper-heavy, and prone to fraud. UniTrust Ledger solves this by creating a "Single Source of Truth." By anchoring student data onto the blockchain, we transform academic records into immutable digital assets that are globally accessible and 100% tamper-proof.
## 🛠️ Current Features (MVP)
The current iteration focuses on the core logic of academic integrity:On-Chain Academic Storage: Directly records GPA and extracurricular points onto the Stellar ledger.Authorized Administrative Minting: Utilizes admin.require_auth() to ensure only verified educational institutions (Registrar’s Office) can issue or update records.Instant Third-Party Verification: Employers and institutions can verify a student's entire academic history in seconds by querying their public Stellar address.High Efficiency & Low Cost: Leverages Stellar’s consensus mechanism to process thousands of student updates with near-zero transaction fees ($< 0.00001$ USD).
## 🚀 Future Roadmap
UniTrust Ledger aims to become a Comprehensive Talent Ecosystem:Skill-Based Micro-credentials: Automating the issuance of NFTs for specific soft skills (Leadership, AI Literacy, Teamwork) upon activity completion.AI-Driven Career Matching: Integrating AI models to analyze a student's "Skill Wallet" and suggest personalized career paths or internship opportunities.Soulbound Token (SBT) Implementation: Transitioning all credentials to non-transferable SBTs to establish a permanent, non-forgeable "Digital Reputation."Cross-Institutional Standardization: Scaling the protocol to allow multiple universities to participate in a unified, global verification network.## 🎨 Design & VisionThe project follows a Minimalist & Professional aesthetic, utilizing a palette of Medical Blue, Neon Cyan, and Tech Green to evoke a sense of trust, innovation, and "New Frontiers."
## 💡 Why This Matters
As a student from a rural village in Hue now studying AI in Da Nang, I believe technology should level the playing field. UniTrust Ledger ensures that no matter where a student comes from, their hard work is documented in a way that the whole world can trust.
